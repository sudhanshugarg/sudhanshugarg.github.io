I'm an engineer at heart. Over the years, I've learned that what excites me most, is to really understand what is going on. It doesn't matter where, which system, CS, Genetics, Running, GPS systems or something else. Just the joy of understanding how something works. And then, being articulate enough to explain it. To myself, or to others. To revel in that understanding. To see someone else understand what you understood, is incredible. Only once I understand it, can I usually build it. Even then, if its broken, I can seldom fix it, but it doesn't hurt to try.
